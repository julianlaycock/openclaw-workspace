Concept B — Dark Mode (Landing Page Harmony)
COHESION

Full dark theme matching the landing page's #2D3333 palette. Accent colors (#C5E0EE, #E8A87C) carry through. Best visual cohesion between marketing site and product. Modern, fintech feel. Compliance managers may find it unusual — but memorable.
