- **Application effort:** 10–15 hours (straightforward online form)

### 3. Sovereign Tech Fund ⭐ WORTH ATTEMPTING — LONG SHOT
- **Funder:** Sovereign Tech Agency (German government, BMWK-adjacent)
- **Amount:** €50K minimum, typically €150K–€500K+
- **Deadline:** Rolling applications
- **Eligibility fit:** ⚠️ MEDIUM-WEAK — They fund **established, widely-used** open source infrastructure (think: curl, OpenSSL, Python packaging). `open-annex-iv` is too new/niche currently.
- **What would make it viable:** If `open-annex-iv` gains significant adoption (20+ dependent packages, used by multiple companies). Not there yet.
- **Requirements:** FOSS licensed ✅, must be "open digital base technologies" — regulatory XML libraries are borderline
- **Caelith benefit:** Massive funding if accepted
- **Application effort:** 15–20 hours
- **Stackable:** NO — cannot combine with other public funding for same work
- **⚡ ACTION: Revisit in 6–12 months once open-annex-iv has traction. Not viable now.**
