## Solution Section

### Headline
**One platform. Every rule. Always current.**

### Body
Caelith encodes European fund regulations into automated checks that run in seconds. When something fails, you know exactly why — and what to do about it. When auditors ask for proof, you hand them a timestamped evidence bundle, not a folder of PDFs.

### Capabilities Grid

#### ⚡ Rule Engine
13 rules across UCITS, AIFMD, EuVECA, ELTIF, and MMF. 87+ automated tests. Run a full compliance check in seconds — results include pass/fail status with direct regulatory citations.

#### 🧭 Readiness Assessment
See exactly how prepared you are. 70 compliance controls mapped, scored, and prioritised. Know your gaps before the regulator finds them.

#### 🤖 Compliance Copilot
Ask any regulatory question in plain language. Get sourced answers, contextual explanations, and suggested next steps. Like having a regulatory expert on call — without the hourly rate.

#### 👁️ Delegation Monitoring
Outsourced functions still need oversight. Track, document, and evidence your supervisory arrangements for delegated activities under AIFMD and UCITS.

#### 💧 Liquidity Management Tool Tracking
Document your LMT configurations — swing pricing, redemption gates, anti-dilution levies. Stay ready for the post-2024 regulatory requirements.

#### 🔍 Screening
Automated checks against sanctions lists, PEP databases, and adverse media. Results logged with timestamps, feeding directly into your audit trail.

## How It Works

### Step 1: Set up your fund
Choose your structure (UCITS, AIF, EuVECA, ELTIF, or MMF), jurisdiction, and management company. Caelith auto-applies the right regulatory framework. **5 fund structures, 5 jurisdictions.**

### Step 2: Run compliance checks
One click. 87+ tests. Results in seconds. Every finding links to the specific regulation, shows the data evaluated, and suggests remediation.

### Step 3: Generate evidence
Annex IV reports, compliance summaries, or full evidence bundles — all timestamped, cross-referenced, and audit-ready. Hand them over with confidence.
