# Lanzadera Meeting — Feb 24, 2026

## What Lanzadera Is
- Spain's top startup incubator, founded by **Juan Roig** (Mercadona, €4B+ revenue)
- Not a customer. Not a VC. An **incubator/accelerator** — they provide resources, network, mentorship
- They want to see: real product, founder conviction, market understanding, velocity

## The Frame
**This is a conversation, not a pitch.** You're a founder showing what you've built and exploring whether Lanzadera is the right partner for the next phase. Don't over-demo. Don't sell. Be direct about where you are and where you're going.

## 15-Minute Structure

### 0:00–2:00 — Context (YOU talk, no screen)
- "I'm Julian, CEO of Caelith. We automate regulatory compliance for EU fund managers."
- "AIFMD II takes effect April 2026. Every fund manager in Europe needs to comply. Most are using consultants and spreadsheets."
- "I built this in 3 months. Solo founder. The product is live."
- **Key stat:** "We've contacted 10 German KVGs. The market is 100+ just in Germany, thousands across Europe."

### 2:00–8:00 — Live Product Demo (SHOW, don't tell)
Open www.caelith.tech, log in with demo@caelith.com / Demo1234!

**Flow** (match the video script — you know this cold):
1. **Dashboard** (1 min) — 5 funds, €812M AUM, compliance scores at a glance
2. **RAIF fund detail** (1.5 min) — Norges Bank 55% concentration, delegation risk, leverage
3. **Rule engine** (1 min) — 13 codified rules, pass/fail, legal paragraph references
4. **Readiness Assessment** (1 min) — 71% ready, red on delegation substance
5. **Annex IV report** (30s) — "Two hours of work, thirty seconds"
6. **Copilot** (1 min) — "Which funds have fewer than two LMTs?" → finds the ELTIF gap

**DON'T show:** Screening (too granular for incubator), evidence bundle (too technical), transfers (not impressive enough)

### 8:00–11:00 — Market & Business
- **Market:** 100+ KVGs in Germany, 3,000+ AIFMs across EU. All must comply by April 2026.
- **Pricing:** €990–€3,500/mo. "93% cheaper than the Big 4 consultants charging €200K+ for manual compliance projects."
- **Traction:** 10 KVGs contacted, 1 warm rejection ("interesting case, next round"), product live at caelith.tech
- **Business model:** SaaS subscription. Land with AIFMD II, expand to AML (July 2027), SFDR, ELTIF 2.0
- **Competition:** Big 4 consultants (manual, expensive), RegTech players (generic, not AIFMD II specific), Excel (free, terrible)

### 11:00–13:00 — Why Now, Why Me
- **Why now:** AIFMD II deadline is a forcing function. Every fund manager in Europe HAS to act. Regulatory pressure creates urgency — this isn't optional.
- **Why me:** I understand both the tech and the regulation. Built the entire platform — frontend, backend, rule engine, AI copilot — in 3 months. Solo. That's founder velocity.
- **Why Lanzadera:** Access to European network, credibility, mentorship on GTM. I can build product. I need help scaling distribution.

### 13:00–15:00 — The Ask + Q&A
- "I'm looking for the right partner to scale distribution across Europe. Lanzadera's network and ecosystem could accelerate that significantly."
- Open for questions. Be honest about what you don't know.

## What NOT To Do

- **Don't over-demo.** 6 minutes of product, max. They get it fast.
- **Don't get technical.** No mention of "Next.js", "PostgreSQL", "RLS". They care about the problem and the market.
- **Don't be defensive about zero revenue.** Own it. "Product launched recently. Look at what one person built in 3 months — that's the velocity."
- **Don't ask for money.** This is an incubator conversation. Ask for partnership, resources, network.
- **Don't compare to specific competitors by name.** Just frame the landscape.
