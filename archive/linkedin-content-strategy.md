# LinkedIn Content Strategy — Caelith

## Posting Schedule
**Frequency:** 1x per day, Monday–Friday
**Time:** 8:15 AM CET (optimal for DACH financial services professionals — they check LinkedIn with morning coffee before 9 AM meetings)
**Alternative slot:** Tuesday/Thursday add a 2nd post at 12:15 PM CET (lunch scroll)

## Why NOT 2x/day
- LinkedIn algorithm penalizes high-frequency posting from the same account (suppresses reach of post #2 if post #1 is <18h old)
- 1 strong post/day > 2 mediocre ones
- Exception: Tuesday + Thursday can handle a lunch post IF the morning post was a quick take (not long-form)

## Content Pillars (rotate weekly)

### Pillar 1: AIFMD II Countdown (Mon/Wed)
Urgency-driven. "X days until the deadline" series.
- Specific compliance gaps most KVGs haven't thought about
- Real examples from the regulation (cite articles)
- "Did you know?" format — short, punchy, one insight

### Pillar 2: Build in Public (Tue/Thu)
Show the product, show the journey.
- Screenshots of Caelith features (blur sensitive data)
- "We just shipped X" updates
- Founder lessons — what you're learning building in RegTech
- HWR Berlin / grant journey (people love underdog stories)

### Pillar 3: Industry Insight (Fri)
Thought leadership. Position Julian as the compliance-tech voice.
- Regulatory news commentary (ESMA, BaFin, CSSF)
- "The real cost of manual compliance" (data-backed)
- Competitor landscape analysis (without naming — "most solutions cost €100K+")
- EU regulatory fragmentation takes

## Format Mix
| Format | Frequency | Why |
|--------|-----------|-----|
| Text-only (hot take, 3-5 lines) | 2x/week | Highest organic reach on LinkedIn |
| Text + image/screenshot | 2x/week | Product visibility |
| Carousel (slides) | 1x/week | Deep engagement, saves + shares |
| Poll | 1x/2 weeks | Algorithm boost, market research |
| Video | 1x/month | Low priority until >500 followers |

## Hashtag Strategy
Use 3-5 per post, mix broad + niche:
- Always: #AIFMD #RegTech #Compliance
- Rotate: #Fondsmanagement #KVG #BaFin #KAGB #FinTech #StartupBerlin #GründerLeben
- Never: #AI #Innovation #Disruption (generic, low signal)

## Paid Ads: NOT YET
**Recommendation: Don't pay for LinkedIn ads right now.**
- LinkedIn ads cost €5-15 per click for B2B financial services
- At your stage, €500/month in ads = maybe 50-100 clicks = maybe 1-2 conversations
- That same €500 in time spent on organic content + direct outreach = 5-10 conversations
- **Revisit paid ads when:** >500 followers, >3 paying customers, landing page has case study/social proof
- **Exception:** If a post goes semi-viral (>5K impressions), boost it for €50-100 to ride the wave

## Engagement Rules (daily, 10 min)
- Comment on 3-5 posts from compliance/fund management professionals BEFORE posting
- Reply to every comment on your posts within 2 hours
- Connect with people who engage (like/comment) on your posts
- Follow and engage with: BaFin official, ESMA, BVI, BAI, German KVG compliance heads
