Pricing Transparency

anevis operates on custom enterprise pricing, typically ranging from €10,000 to €50,000+ per year depending on the scope of services and number of funds. This reflects their comprehensive service model — you get a dedicated team, managed infrastructure, and a broad product suite.

Caelith takes a different approach: transparent per-fund pricing starting at €99/fund/month. You pay for what you use, scale up or down freely, and there's no long-term contract. For a KVG with 10 funds, that's roughly €12K/year for a purpose-built Annex IV solution — a fraction of enterprise alternatives.

API-First vs Managed Service

This is the fundamental architectural difference. anevis provides a managed service: your data goes in, their team processes it, reports come out. It works well for firms that want to outsource regulatory reporting entirely.

Caelith provides a platform: a REST API and dashboard that your team controls directly. You upload fund data (or pipe it from your PMS via API), Caelith validates and generates the XML, and you submit it. No tickets, no waiting, no vendor dependency for routine operations.

For firms with in-house tech capability — even a single developer — the API-first model means Annex IV generation can be fully automated as part of your quarterly close process.

Scope & Focus

anevis offers a broad regulatory reporting suite: Annex IV, factsheets, PRIIPs KIDs, risk calculations (VaR, stress testing), and more. If you need all of that from one vendor, anevis delivers.
