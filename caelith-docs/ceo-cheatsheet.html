2. The Problem

AIFMD II — EU's updated rulebook for alternative investment funds. Germany's transposition: FRiG (amends KAGB).

Deadline: April 16, 2026. Miss it → fines, license risk, lawsuits.

Today's workflow: Compliance officers manually fill XML/Excel (15–30 hrs/filing). Consultants charge €3–5K per filing or €150–200K/yr. BaFin rejects errors — no pre-validation exists.

The gap: 47% of fund managers unprepared. New regime is 3× more complex.

💬 "Right now, a compliance officer at a small German KVG spends two days filling in an XML template by hand. One typo and BaFin rejects the filing. We eliminate that entire process."

10. Objection Handling

"Isn't this just a dashboard?"
The dashboard is a reference implementation. The core product is API infrastructure — filing pipelines, validation, screening. Fund admins want APIs they embed. That's where scale lives.
"How is this different from anevis?"
anevis = managed service for enterprises, €15–50K. We're self-service infrastructure at 10× lower cost. They're Accenture, we're AWS.
"Do KVGs even buy software?"
AIFMD II changes the math: 3× more data points, stricter validation. Excel doesn't scale. Digital reporting makes software inevitable.
"Solo founder risk"
EXIST for structured support, HWR + HTW for academic backing, open-core = community becomes the team. Plus Lanzadera.
"Too early for us"
Completely understand. What specific milestones would make us interesting for a future conversation?
"What about risk calculations?"
anevis does VaR/DV01. AIFMD II's new reqs are delegation disclosures, LMT docs, loan origination reporting. Those are our focus. Risk calc is Phase 2.
"Will ESMA delay the deadline?"
~30% chance. But current Annex IV is already manual and painful. We solve today's problem AND tomorrow's.
