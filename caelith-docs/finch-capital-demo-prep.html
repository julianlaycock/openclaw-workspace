3. Anticipated Questions + Answers

Q: Why haven't you closed any customers yet?
We've pivoted outreach — stopped info@ emails, now targeting compliance officers directly via LinkedIn. 10 connections sent today. Henrik's response validates the thesis: KVGs outsource to expensive consultants — that's our market. We also see fund administrators as a channel — they serve multiple KVGs and need scalable tooling.
Q: What's your competitive landscape?
anevis solutions is the incumbent — enterprise, managed service, 6-figure contracts. They validate the market. We're self-service, API-first, targeting the mid-market that can't afford anevis and currently uses consultants + Excel. Think Stripe vs. enterprise payment processors.
Q: How do you know KVGs will buy software vs. keep using consultants?
Two forces: (1) AIFMD II significantly expands reporting requirements — consultants don't scale when you need ongoing compliance, not annual audits. (2) Regulators are moving toward machine-readable reporting (Annex IV XML). You can't submit XML by hiring a lawyer. The shift to digital reporting makes software inevitable.
Q: What's your GTM?
Three channels: (1) Free readiness check as inbound funnel, (2) Direct outreach to KVG compliance officers via LinkedIn, (3) Fund administrators as a distribution channel — one admin serves 10–50 funds. We're in HWR Berlin's Startup Incubator and Lanzadera (European expansion). LinkedIn company page live.
Q: Why are you the team to build this?
(Julian — prep your personal answer. Emphasize: domain learning speed, technical execution, shipping 32 public API endpoints as a solo founder, incubator backing from HWR Berlin + Lanzadera.)
Q: What would you raise and when?
We're focused on getting to first paying customers before raising. When we do, it'll be a pre-seed/seed to scale GTM in DACH. Happy to keep you updated as we hit milestones.
Q: How defensible is this?
Three layers: (1) Structured regulatory dataset (27 NCA profiles, 14 quirks, calendars, rule mappings, regulatory intelligence feed from ESMA/BaFin) — no one else has this in API form. (2) Network effects from fund admin channel — more funds = better data. (3) Switching costs once embedded in compliance workflows. Plus Python + TypeScript SDKs, webhooks with HMAC signing.
