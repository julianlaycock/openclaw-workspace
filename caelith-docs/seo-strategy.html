Q: What is AIFMD II? A: AIFMD II (Directive 2024/927/EU) is the revision of the EU Alternative Investment Fund Managers Directive. It introduces new requirements for delegation, liquidity management, loan origination, and regulatory reporting. Enforcement begins April 16, 2026.

Q: When is the AIFMD II deadline? A: The AIFMD II enforcement deadline is April 16, 2026. All EU AIFMs must comply with the new requirements by this date.

Q: What is Annex IV reporting? A: Annex IV is the standardized AIFMD reporting template. Fund managers must file XML reports with their national regulator (BaFin, CSSF, AMF, etc.) containing fund data validated against ESMA's XSD schema.

Q: Is Caelith free? A: Caelith's core is open source (Apache 2.0). The readiness check and Annex IV XML validation are free. Advanced features like automated sanctions screening and audit trails are available in paid plans.

Q: Which regulators does Caelith support? A: Caelith supports filing with BaFin (Germany), CSSF (Luxembourg), FMA (Austria), AMF (France), CBI (Ireland), and other EU national competent authorities. All Annex IV output is ESMA XSD-validated.
