Under § 293 KAGB, semi-professional investors in a Spezial-AIF must meet the following requirements:
  • Minimum investment of €200,000 (or equivalent)
  • Written declaration of risk awareness
  • KYC/AML verification completed and valid
  • Cannot exceed concentration limits per fund structure
