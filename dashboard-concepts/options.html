Philosophy: Compliance officers open the dashboard to act, not to admire charts. Action Queue is the hero — it's the largest element, immediately visible. KPIs are stacked vertically on the right as reference context, not the focal point. Fund cards get their own row at 3-col for at-a-glance fund health. Analytics and decisions share equal weight at the bottom since they're investigative, not urgent. Best for: daily operations, "what do I need to fix right now?" workflow.

Philosophy: Funds ARE the dashboard. Each fund is an expanded mini-dashboard showing capacity, KYC health, risk flags, and last decision inline. No need to click through to a fund detail page for the 80% case. Funds with issues auto-expand; clean funds can collapse to a single row. This maps to how a compliance officer actually thinks: "How is Fund X doing? Fund Y?" — not "Show me a chart." Best for: KVGs with 3-15 funds where per-fund context is critical. Scales poorly above ~20 funds.
