# Frequently Asked Questions

---

### 1. What is Caelith?

Caelith is a compliance automation platform built for investment fund managers, KVGs, and ManCos. It automates regulatory checks, generates audit-ready reports, and provides an AI-powered Copilot that answers compliance questions in plain language. Think of it as your always-on compliance analyst — one that never misses a rule change.

---

### 2. What regulations does Caelith cover?

Caelith covers the major European fund regulations including UCITS, AIFMD, EuVECA, ELTIF, and Money Market Fund rules. We support 13 regulatory rules across 5 jurisdictions (Germany, Luxembourg, Ireland, France, and Spain), with 70 compliance controls mapped and 87+ automated tests. Coverage is expanding continuously.

---

### 3. How is my data secured?

Security is foundational, not an afterthought. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We're pursuing ISO 27001 certification and follow industry best practices for access control, logging, and infrastructure security. Your fund data is isolated per tenant — no shared databases, no cross-contamination.

---

### 4. Can I import existing investor and fund data?

Yes. Caelith supports CSV bulk import for investors, fund structures, and historical data. Download our import templates, map your columns, and upload. The system validates your data before import, flagging any issues so you can fix them before they enter the system.

---

### 5. What does Caelith cost?

We offer tiered pricing based on the number of funds and features you need. Contact us at sales@caelith.com or book a demo through our website for a personalised quote. We also offer a trial period so you can evaluate the platform with your actual data before committing.

---

### 6. Do I need technical skills to use Caelith?

Not at all. Caelith is designed for compliance officers, fund managers, and legal teams — not developers. The interface is point-and-click, reports generate with one button, and the Copilot explains everything in plain language. If you can use a spreadsheet, you can use Caelith.

---

### 7. How does the Compliance Copilot work?

The Copilot is an AI assistant trained on European fund regulation. You can ask it questions like "Does my fund need an Annex IV filing?" or "What are the UCITS concentration limits?" and get clear, sourced answers. It also appears contextually — when you're reviewing a compliance finding, it can explain the issue and suggest remediation steps. It's like having a regulatory expert on call 24/7.

---

### 8. What's the audit trail?

Every action in Caelith is logged: who did it, when, and what changed. Compliance checks record the exact rules evaluated, the data used, and the results. Reports capture their generation timestamp and the underlying data snapshot. This gives you a complete, immutable audit trail for regulators, auditors, and internal governance.

---

### 9. Can I export my data and reports?

Absolutely. Reports export as PDF. Investor data, compliance results, and fund information can be exported as CSV. Evidence Bundles package everything together for regulatory submissions or auditor requests. Your data is yours — we make it easy to get it out.

---

### 10. Is Caelith GDPR compliant?

Yes. We process data in accordance with GDPR. Investor personal data is handled with appropriate safeguards, access controls, and retention policies. We provide data processing agreements (DPAs) and can support your data protection impact assessments. You can also configure data retention periods per fund.
