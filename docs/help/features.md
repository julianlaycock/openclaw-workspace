# Caelith Features

A complete overview of every capability in the platform.

---

## Rule Engine

The core of Caelith. The Rule Engine encodes 13 regulatory rules across UCITS, AIFMD, EuVECA, ELTIF, and Money Market Fund frameworks into 87+ automated tests. When you run a compliance check, the engine evaluates your fund data against every applicable rule simultaneously, returning pass/warning/fail results with full citation to the underlying regulation. Rules are maintained and updated by our regulatory team — you never need to manually track rule changes.

## Readiness Assessment

Before you face a regulator, know where you stand. The Readiness Assessment maps your fund against 70 compliance controls and gives you a clear score showing how prepared you are. It identifies gaps — missing policies, incomplete documentation, untested procedures — and prioritises them by regulatory impact. Think of it as a pre-flight checklist for your next audit or regulatory review.

## Compliance Copilot

An AI-powered assistant that speaks regulation fluently so you don't have to. Ask the Copilot any compliance question — "What are the leverage limits for my AIFMD fund?" or "Do I need to report under Annex IV?" — and get a clear, sourced answer. It also appears contextually alongside compliance findings, offering plain-language explanations and suggested remediation steps. Available 24/7, no appointment needed.

## Delegation Monitoring

When you outsource fund functions — portfolio management, risk, administration — you're still responsible. Delegation Monitoring helps you document, track, and evidence your oversight of delegated activities. Define your delegation arrangements, set review schedules, and maintain the paper trail that proves you're meeting your supervisory obligations under AIFMD and UCITS.

## Liquidity Management Tool (LMT) Tracking

Post-2024 regulatory updates require funds to document their liquidity management tools and activation thresholds. Caelith tracks your LMT configurations — swing pricing, redemption gates, anti-dilution levies — and ensures they're documented, reviewed, and ready for regulatory inspection. No more scrambling to prove your liquidity framework exists when the regulator asks.

## Screening

Automated screening checks your investors and counterparties against sanctions lists, PEP databases, and adverse media sources. Results are logged with timestamps and evidence, feeding directly into your compliance audit trail. Configure screening frequency and thresholds to match your risk appetite and regulatory requirements.

## Annex IV Reporting

AIFMD Annex IV reports are complex, repetitive, and error-prone when done manually. Caelith auto-generates Annex IV reports from your fund data, pre-filling the required fields and flagging any missing information before submission. Review, adjust if needed, and export — cutting what used to take days into minutes.

## Evidence Bundle

When a regulator or auditor asks "prove it," you need more than a summary. The Evidence Bundle packages your compliance check results, underlying data, policies, and audit logs into a single, downloadable archive. Everything is timestamped and cross-referenced. Hand it over with confidence — it's the complete picture, not a curated highlight reel.

## Audit Trail

Every action in Caelith is recorded: who did it, when, and what changed. Compliance checks log the exact rules evaluated, the data inputs, and the results. Report generation captures the data snapshot used. User actions — edits, approvals, exports — are all timestamped. The audit trail is immutable and always accessible, giving you a defensible record for any regulatory inquiry.

## Dashboard

Your compliance command centre. The Dashboard shows real-time status across all your funds: readiness scores, recent compliance check results, upcoming deadlines, and flagged issues. Filter by fund, jurisdiction, or regulation. Drill into any metric to see the underlying detail. It's designed to give you — and your board — a clear answer to "are we compliant?" in under 10 seconds.
