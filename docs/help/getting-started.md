# Getting Started with Caelith

Welcome to Caelith — your compliance automation platform for investment fund regulation. This guide walks you through your first session, from login to your first compliance report.

**Time to complete:** ~15 minutes

---

## Step 1: Log In

1. Open your browser and go to **app.caelith.com**
2. Enter the email and password from your welcome email
3. Complete two-factor authentication (we recommend an authenticator app)
4. You'll land on your **Dashboard** — your compliance command centre

> 💡 First time? Check your inbox for the activation email. If you don't see it, check spam or contact support@caelith.com.

---

## Step 2: Create Your First Fund

1. Click **"+ New Fund"** from the Dashboard
2. Fill in the basics:
   - **Fund name** (e.g., "Global Equity Fund I")
   - **Fund structure** — choose from: UCITS, AIF, EuVECA, ELTIF, or Money Market Fund
   - **Jurisdiction** — select your primary jurisdiction (Germany, Luxembourg, Ireland, France, or Spain)
   - **Management company** — your KVG or ManCo
3. Click **Create Fund**

Your fund now appears on the Dashboard with a compliance readiness score of 0%. Let's fix that.

---

## Step 3: Add Investors

You can add investors manually or import them in bulk.

### Manual Entry
1. Navigate to **Investors** from the left sidebar
2. Click **"+ Add Investor"**
3. Enter investor details: name, type (institutional/retail/professional), jurisdiction, investment amount
4. Click **Save**

### Bulk Import
1. Go to **Investors → Import**
2. Download the CSV template
3. Fill in your investor data and upload
4. Review the preview, resolve any flagged issues, and confirm

---

## Step 4: Run Your First Compliance Check

This is where Caelith does the heavy lifting.

1. Open your fund and click **"Run Compliance Check"**
2. Caelith's Rule Engine evaluates your fund against all applicable regulations — across 13 regulatory rules and 87+ automated tests
3. Wait a few seconds. You'll see:
   - ✅ **Pass** — requirement met
   - ⚠️ **Warning** — potential issue, review recommended
   - ❌ **Fail** — action required
4. Click any result to see the specific rule, the evidence evaluated, and recommended remediation

> 💡 Not sure what a finding means? Click **"Ask Copilot"** next to any result for a plain-language explanation and suggested next steps.

---

## Step 5: Review Your Readiness Assessment

1. Go to **Readiness** from the sidebar
2. You'll see an overall readiness score and a breakdown by regulation area
3. Each control maps to one of 70 compliance controls — click into any to see what's needed
4. Use the Copilot to help you draft missing policies or documents

---

## Step 6: Generate Your First Report

1. Go to **Reports** from the sidebar
2. Choose your report type:
   - **Compliance Summary** — overview of current status
   - **Annex IV (AIFMD)** — regulatory filing report
   - **Evidence Bundle** — full audit package for regulators or auditors
3. Select the fund and reporting period
4. Click **Generate**
5. Download as PDF or export directly

Every report includes a full audit trail — who ran it, when, and what data was used.
