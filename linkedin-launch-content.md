### About Section

Caelith is a compliance platform built specifically for EU fund managers navigating AIFMD II, ELTIF 2.0, and the growing wave of regulatory reporting requirements.

If you manage alternative investment funds in Europe, you already know the problem: compliance is manual, fragmented, and expensive. Teams spend weeks preparing Annex IV reports, cross-referencing leverage calculations, and chasing data across spreadsheets and legacy systems. When regulations change — as they did with AIFMD II, effective April 16, 2026 — the scramble starts all over again.

Caelith replaces that scramble with a single platform. We ingest your fund data, map it against current regulatory frameworks, and generate submission-ready reports. Our Compliance Copilot — an AI assistant trained on your actual portfolio data — lets you ask natural-language questions about your fund's regulatory status and get precise, sourced answers in seconds.

We built Caelith because we lived the problem. Our founder spent years in enterprise consulting and regulatory advisory, watching fund managers burn time and money on compliance workflows that should have been automated a decade ago. The technology finally caught up. So we built it.

Caelith is designed for AIFMs, KVGs, fund administrators, and compliance teams managing regulated fund structures across the EU. Whether you're a €200M single-fund manager or a multi-billion-euro platform, Caelith scales to your reporting obligations.

Based in Berlin. Built for Europe. Shipping fast.

### Post 3 — Problem Awareness (AIFMD II Deadline)

April 16, 2026.

That's the AIFMD II transposition deadline. And most fund managers I talk to aren't ready.

Here's what's changing:

📋 **New Annex IV reporting templates** — expanded fields, new data points, stricter validation. Your old templates won't work.

📊 **Enhanced leverage reporting** — two methods (Gross & Commitment), both mandatory, with new decomposition requirements.

💧 **Liquidity risk management** — new disclosure obligations, stress testing requirements, and investor-level liquidity matching.

🏛️ **Delegation & substance rules** — tighter oversight of delegated portfolio management. "Letterbox entities" are officially on notice.

This affects 3,500+ AIFMs across the EU. Every KVG in Germany. Every FIA manager in France. Every QIAIF in Ireland.

Non-compliance isn't theoretical. It means:

→ Rejected filings with your NCA
