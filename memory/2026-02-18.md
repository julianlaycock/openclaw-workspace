## Pricing Strategy Overhaul
- **Old pricing was gut-feel, not validated**: €299/€799/Custom
- **Problem**: €299/mo signals "toy" to compliance officers spending €200K on Deloitte
- **Research findings**: No competitor publishes pricing. FundApps ~€25-80K ACV, Kneip ~€15-50K, Regnology €50-500K+. No sub-€10K compliance SaaS in the market.
- **New value-based pricing**:
  - Essentials: €990/mo (3 funds, 200 investors) — ACV €11,880
  - Professional: €1,990/mo (15 funds, unlimited) — ACV €23,880
  - Enterprise: From €3,500/mo (unlimited, on-prem, SSO) — ACV €42,000+
- **Key framing**: "93% cheaper than Big 4" (not "50× cheaper" — sounds less credible)
