Key Assumptions:
• Pilots convert at 80% rate (3-5 pilots → 3-4 paying customers)
• Average sales cycle: 4-6 weeks for Starter/Professional, 8-12 weeks for Enterprise
• CTO hired Month 2 at €70K/yr base (below market, compensated with equity)
• No additional engineering hires in 18-month window (CTO + founder sufficient)
• Churn: 5% annual (compliance tools are sticky — regulatory obligations don't decrease)
• Expansion revenue: customers upgrade as they add funds (included in customer count growth)
• SOC 2 Type II + ISO 27001 pursued in parallel, Month 8-14
• Series A target: Month 18 at €264K+ ARR, 30+ customers, with 8+ months runway remaining
• No pricing increases modeled (conservative — premium features can command higher pricing)
• Germany-only for M1-6, DACH expansion M7-12, broader EU M13-18
