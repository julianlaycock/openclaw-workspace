The problem
There is no self-service SaaS for AIF compliance.
3,500+ EU alternative investment fund managers must classify investors, validate thresholds, generate regulatory reports, and prove every decision to regulators — across AIFMD II, KAGB, ELTIF 2.0, and more. Today, they do all of this with spreadsheets, email chains, and Big 4 consultants. Enterprise tools like anevis exist but cost €100K+ and require a human account manager. The mid-market — 600+ small and mid-size KVGs — has nothing. Every compliance decision is trapped in PDFs and tribal knowledge, invisible to auditors and impossible to scale.
