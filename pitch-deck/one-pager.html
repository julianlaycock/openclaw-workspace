The problem

EU alternative investment fund managers spend €150–200K/year on Big 4 consultants to manually classify investors, validate regulatory thresholds, and generate compliance reports — using spreadsheets, email chains, and PDFs. The solution

Caelith automates investor classification, regulatory validation, and audit reporting across 6 EU frameworks — AIFMD II, KAGB, ELTIF 2.0, RAIF, SIF, and Annex IV. Every decision is cryptographically hash-chained, creating tamper-proof proof for regulators. Full audit in under 4 minutes. Starting at €299/month — 50× cheaper than consultants.
