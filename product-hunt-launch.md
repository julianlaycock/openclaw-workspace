## Description

Caelith automates regulatory compliance for investment funds. 13 rules, 5 jurisdictions, 87+ tests, one platform. AI Copilot explains regulations in plain language. Built for compliance officers who are tired of spreadsheets. (224 characters)

---

## 3 Key Features

### 1. 🤖 AI Compliance Copilot
Ask any regulatory question and get a sourced, plain-language answer. The Copilot also explains compliance findings contextually and suggests remediation — like having a regulatory expert available 24/7.

### 2. ⚡ Automated Rule Engine
87+ tests across UCITS, AIFMD, EuVECA, ELTIF, and Money Market Fund regulations. Run a full compliance check in seconds, not days. Results include pass/fail status with direct citations to the underlying regulation.

### 3. 📦 Audit-Ready Evidence Bundles
One click generates a complete, timestamped package of compliance results, supporting data, and audit logs. Hand it to your regulator or auditor with confidence — everything is cross-referenced and immutable.
