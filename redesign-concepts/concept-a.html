Frequently asked questions

What regulatory frameworks does Caelith cover?+
AIFMD II, KAGB (including A1(19)), ELTIF 2.0, RAIF Law, SIF Law, and relevant provisions of MiFID II. 13 pre-built rules across 6 frameworks. Annex IV XML validated against ESMA's XSD schema (Rev 6 v1.2).
How does the hash-chained audit trail work?+
Every compliance decision is stored as a block containing the decision data, a SHA-256 hash, and the hash of the previous block. This creates a tamper-evident chain — any modification is immediately detectable.
Can I integrate Caelith with existing systems?+
Yes. Caelith provides a RESTful API for integration with transfer agents, fund admin platforms, and portfolio management systems. Enterprise plans include custom integration support.
When does AIFMD II take effect?+
AIFMD II must be transposed into national law by April 16, 2026. Fund managers need to be fully compliant by this date.
Is my data secure?+
Caelith is hosted on EU-based infrastructure (Frankfurt), with end-to-end encryption and GDPR compliance. Data never leaves the EU. Enterprise customers can opt for on-premise deployment.
