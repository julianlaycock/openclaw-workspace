Frequently asked questions

What regulatory frameworks does Caelith cover?+
AIFMD II, KAGB (A1(19)), ELTIF 2.0, RAIF Law, SIF Law, and MiFID II provisions. 13 rules across 6 frameworks. Annex IV XML validated against ESMA XSD Rev 6 v1.2.
How does the audit trail work?+
Every decision is stored as a block with SHA-256 hash linked to the previous block. Creates a tamper-evident chain — modifications are immediately detectable.
Can I integrate with existing systems?+
Yes. RESTful API for transfer agents, fund admin, and portfolio management. Enterprise plans include custom integration support.
When does AIFMD II take effect?+
April 16, 2026. Fund managers must be fully compliant by this date.
Is my data secure?+
EU-based hosting (Frankfurt), end-to-end encryption, GDPR compliant. Data never leaves the EU. On-premise available for Enterprise.
