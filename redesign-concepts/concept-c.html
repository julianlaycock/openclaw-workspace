FAQ

Common questions.

What regulatory frameworks does Caelith cover?+
AIFMD II, KAGB (A1(19)), ELTIF 2.0, RAIF Law, SIF Law, and MiFID II provisions. 13 rules across 6 frameworks. Annex IV XML validated against ESMA XSD Rev 6 v1.2.
How does the audit trail work?+
Every decision is stored as a block with SHA-256 hash linked to the previous block. Any modification is immediately detectable — tamper-evident by design.
Can I integrate with existing systems?+
Yes. RESTful API for transfer agents, fund admin, and portfolio management systems. Enterprise plans include custom integration support.
When does AIFMD II take effect?+
April 16, 2026. Fund managers must be fully compliant by this date.
Is my data secure?+
EU-hosted infrastructure (Frankfurt), end-to-end encryption, GDPR compliant. Data never leaves the EU.
