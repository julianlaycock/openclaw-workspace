Common questions.

What is AIFMD II and when does it take effect?+
AIFMD II is the updated Alternative Investment Fund Managers Directive, expanding regulatory requirements for EU fund managers. The deadline is in 56 days, requiring updated compliance processes, enhanced reporting, and documented audit trails.
How does the hash-chained audit trail work?+
Every compliance decision is recorded with a SHA-256 hash that includes the previous decision's hash, creating a tamper-proof chain. Any modification to historical records would break the chain, providing cryptographic proof of integrity.
Can Caelith integrate with our existing systems?+
Yes. Caelith offers a REST API and webhooks for integration with portfolio management systems, fund administration platforms, and reporting tools. Enterprise plans include custom integration support.
Is our data hosted in the EU?+
Yes. All data is processed and stored within EU-hosted infrastructure. Caelith is fully GDPR-compliant with data residency guarantees.
What does "human-in-the-loop" mean?+
While Caelith automates compliance evaluation, critical decisions can be flagged for human review. The system provides recommendations with full evidence, and authorized users can approve, override, or escalate â€” all logged in the audit trail.
