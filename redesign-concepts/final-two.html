Frequently asked questions

+ What frameworks does Caelith cover?

Caelith currently covers AIFMD II, UCITS VI, SFDR, EU Taxonomy, DORA, and MiFID II. Our rules engine is continuously updated as regulatory guidance evolves, with new rules deployed within 48 hours of publication.

+ How does the hash-chain audit trail work?

Every compliance evaluation generates a SHA-256 hash that references the previous record, creating a tamper-evident chain. If any historical record is modified, the chain breaks — providing cryptographic proof of data integrity to regulators.

+ Can I integrate with existing systems?

Yes. Caelith offers REST APIs and pre-built connectors for major fund administrators, custodians, and portfolio management systems. Most integrations are live within 5 business days.

+ What happens after the free evaluation?

After your 14-day evaluation, you choose a plan that fits your fund count. No lock-in contracts — monthly billing with the flexibility to scale up or down as your AUM changes.

+ Is my data stored in the EU?

All data is processed and stored exclusively in EU data centers (Frankfurt am Main). We are fully GDPR compliant with data processing agreements available for all clients. No data ever leaves EU jurisdiction.
