Frequently asked

What is AIFMD II?
The updated Alternative Investment Fund Managers Directive, effective 2026. It expands reporting, delegation, and liquidity requirements for all EU-regulated fund managers.
How does Caelith help?
We map every article to your fund structure, generate audit-ready documentation, and alert you to regulatory changes before they become compliance gaps.
What's the pricing?
Starting at €299/mo for up to 5 funds. Enterprise plans available for managers with 50+ funds or bespoke reporting needs.
How fast is onboarding?
Most teams are fully operational within 48 hours. We import your fund data, map your regulatory obligations, and generate your first audit report on day one.
