FAQ

Common questions.

What regulatory frameworks does Caelith cover?+
AIFMD II, KAGB (including A1(19)), ELTIF 2.0, RAIF Law, SIF Law, and MiFID II provisions. 13 rules across 6 frameworks. Annex IV XML validated against ESMA XSD Rev 6 v1.2.
Can I try Caelith without signing up?+
Yes. The full demo is available at caelith.tech — no account, no credit card, no forms. Explore the dashboard, run compliance checks, and generate reports immediately.
Do you have an API?+
Yes. Full REST API with OpenAPI/Swagger documentation at /api/docs. Versioned routes (/api/v1/*), API key authentication. Available on Professional and Enterprise plans. The core Annex IV library (open-annex-iv) is open source on npm.
What are EMT/EET/EPT templates?+
European MiFID Template (EMT), ESG Template (EET), and Cost Template (EPT) are standardized regulatory data exchange formats. Caelith auto-generates these from your fund data with 43+ fields pre-filled.
How does the audit trail work?+
Every decision is stored as a block with SHA-256 hash linked to the previous block. Any modification is immediately detectable — tamper-evident by design.
When does AIFMD II take effect?+
April 16, 2026. Fund managers must be fully compliant by this date.
Is my data secure?+
EU-hosted infrastructure (Frankfurt), end-to-end encryption, GDPR compliant. Data never leaves the EU. Enterprise customers can opt for on-premise deployment.
