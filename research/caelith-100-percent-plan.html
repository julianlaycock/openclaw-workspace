What 100/100 Looks Like (and What's Deliberately Left Out)

92/100 is the realistic target at 12 weeks. The remaining 8 points require:

  • SOC 2 Type II certification — 6-12 month process, $30-50K cost. Start after first revenue.
  • ISO 27001 certification — Similar timeline. Docs exist in repo but certification needs third-party audit.
  • 3+ NCA portal integrations — BaFin + CSSF gets you to 85. Adding FMA, AMF, CBI = 95. Full coverage = years.
  • Multi-region deployment — EU data residency with failover. Needed at enterprise scale, not pilot stage.
  • Dedicated security team — Pen testing, bug bounty, WAF tuning. Hire after Series A.

These are not Phase 3 items — they're Phase 4 (post-revenue, post-funding). Don't let perfect be the enemy of shipped.
