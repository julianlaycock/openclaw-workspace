## 2. EU GRANTS

### 2.1 EIC Accelerator 🟢 MAY 2026 BATCH

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Administering body** | European Innovation Council (under Horizon Europe) |
| **Amount** | Up to **€2.5M grant** + up to **€15M equity** investment |
| **Eligibility** | Single SMEs from EU/associated countries. **Solo founders eligible.** Must be a registered company. |
| **Process** | Step 1: Short proposal (5 pages) → GO/NO GO. Step 2: Full proposal (20 pages + video + pitch deck + financials + FTO analysis + 3-min video). Step 3: Remote interview with tech expert. Step 4: Jury interview (top 2.5x budget). Results ~8-9 weeks after batch date. |
| **2026 Batch dates** | ~~Jan 7~~ ~~Mar 4~~ → **May 6** → Jul 8 → Sep 2 → Nov 4 |
| **⚠️ Important** | Mar 4 deadline is for FULL proposals (need prior GO on short proposal). You need to submit a SHORT proposal first — these are accepted rolling and evaluated at the next batch. Submit short proposal ASAP → if GO, submit full to May 6 or Jul 8 batch. |
| **2026 Challenge areas** | Materials, fusion, agriculture, raw materials, climate adaptation. **NO FinTech/RegTech challenge.** You must apply under **"Open" category** (any sector) — this is the most competitive track. |
| **Success rate** | ~5-8% overall. Short proposal GO rate ~30-40%. Open track is MORE competitive than challenge-specific tracks. |
| **Timeline** | ~6-9 months from short proposal to funding decision |
| **Confidence** | **LOW** — no dedicated challenge for FinTech, Open track is ultra-competitive, solo + pre-revenue is a tough profile. But the amounts are transformative and it costs nothing to apply. |
| **What's needed** | Short proposal template (available on F&T portal), clear innovation description, market sizing, team description, IP strategy |
| **⚠️ Key tip** | Many applicants hire EIC consultants (€5-15K). Given your budget, write it yourself — the short proposal is only 5 pages. |
