## ⚠️ Important Notes

**Legal consideration:** The API is for "Verpflichtete" (obliged entities) — KVGs qualify under § 2 Abs. 1 Nr. 7 GwG. Caelith itself may not qualify directly, but as a **software provider acting on behalf of Verpflichtete**, the accreditation path is "Software-Akkreditierung" — exactly what the portal offers.

**Timing:** Accreditation process timeline unknown. Could be days or weeks. Submit ASAP.

**UG status:** "In Gründung" might slow things down. If they require a registered entity, this becomes a dependency on UG incorporation.

**Alternative path:** If accreditation takes too long, individual KVG customers can grant access through their own Transparenzregister accounts and Caelith queries on their behalf via OAuth/delegation.
