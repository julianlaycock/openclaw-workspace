## 11. Risk Mitigation

### Risk: Google algorithm update penalizes new content
**Mitigation:** Focus on quality over quantity. Every piece should genuinely help the reader. Avoid keyword stuffing. Build backlinks from reputable sources.

### Risk: Competitors copy our content
**Mitigation:** Publish faster, go deeper, differentiate with data and case studies they can't replicate.

### Risk: AIFMD II gets delayed (unlikely but possible)
**Mitigation:** Content is still valuable post-deadline. Messaging shifts from "prepare now" to "optimize your process."

### Risk: Low indexing speed (new domain)
**Mitigation:** Submit sitemap, request indexing manually, build backlinks to accelerate crawling.
