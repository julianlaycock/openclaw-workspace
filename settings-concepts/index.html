Settings Page Concepts

4 layout approaches for the Caelith admin settings page — click to preview

1

Vertical Tabs (Sidebar)

Left sidebar with section tabs, content area on the right. Classic settings pattern — familiar and scannable.

Best for deep navigation
2

Horizontal Tabs

Top tabs with two-column card layouts. Modern SaaS feel — uses horizontal space efficiently.

Best for wide screens
3

Single Scrollable Page

All sections stacked vertically with jump links. No hidden content — everything visible at once.

Best for quick scanning
4

Card Grid

Each section as a card in a responsive grid. Dashboard-style overview — great at a glance.

Best for overview
