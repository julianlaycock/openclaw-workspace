Sidebar — Collapsible Items Demo

This is an interactive render of the current Caelith sidebar with collapsible main items.

Both section headers (Portfolio, Compliance, Monitor) and parent items (Funds, Investors, Reports) can be collapsed independently.

Try it:

  • 1. Click section headers (PORTFOLIO, COMPLIANCE, MONITOR) to collapse entire sections
  • 2. Click the chevron on Funds, Investors, or Reports to collapse just their sub-items
  • 3. The main item link still navigates — only the chevron toggles children
  • 4. Numbered badges 3 1 2 show on items with attention needed

Screening fix (also shipped):

  • Dismissing a flagged investor now updates the "Flagged" count in real-time
  • When all matches on an investor are dismissed, their status changes to "Clear" and the summary cards update instantly
  • Confirming a match escalates status to "Confirmed Match"
